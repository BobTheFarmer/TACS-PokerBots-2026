WHAT THIS IS
------------
Build4Good PokerBots 2026, a TACS (Texas A&M) competition sponsored by Jane Street
and Hudson River Trading. Competitors write autonomous Python poker bots.
Bots play head-to-head in a single-elimination tournament bracket.

GAME FORMAT
-----------
- No-limit Texas Hold'em + REDRAW twist
- 2 players, 300 hands per match, 400 chips/hand (chips reset each hand)
- Blinds: SB=1, BB=2
- Streets: 0=preflop, 3=flop, 4=turn, 5=river (note: no 1 or 2)
- Redraw: once per hand, before river (street < 5), you can swap:
    - one of your hole cards ('hole', index 0 or 1), OR
    - one revealed board card ('board', index 0–2 on flop, 0–3 on turn)
  The old card is revealed to the opponent
- Redraw is combined with a betting action in a single RedrawAction(target_type, target_index, action)
- Total time per match: 180s (skeleton config says 60s — may differ in tournament)

SCORING
-------
- Chip wins/losses per hand are summed over 300 hands
- Higher cumulative chips = winner
- Single-elimination bracket

THE UNIQUE CHALLENGE
--------------------
The redraw mechanic is the interesting strategic layer:
- You reveal information when you redraw (opponent sees the discarded card)
- The redraw gives partial information about your hand strength
- Opponent can infer weakness if you redraw a hole card postflop
- Strategic uses: improve a weak hand, manipulate board texture, bluff via redraw timing
- Key decision: is the information you give up worth the card improvement?

API / WHAT TO IMPLEMENT
-----------------------
File to edit: python_skeleton/player.py (implement Player class extending Bot)
Three methods:
  - handle_new_round(game_state, round_state, active) → nothing
  - handle_round_over(game_state, terminal_state, active) → nothing
  - get_action(game_state, round_state, active) → action

Actions available:
  FoldAction()
  CallAction()
  CheckAction()
  RaiseAction(amount)
  RedrawAction(target_type, target_index, action)  ← wraps a betting action

State available at get_action:
  game_state.bankroll      → cumulative chip delta so far
  game_state.game_clock    → seconds remaining
  game_state.round_num     → current hand number
  round_state.street       → 0/3/4/5
  round_state.hands[active]   → your 2 hole cards (strings like "Ah", "Td")
  round_state.board        → community cards
  round_state.pips[active]    → chips you've put in this street
  round_state.stacks[active]  → chips you have left
  round_state.redraws_used[active] → bool, whether you've used your redraw
  round_state.legal_actions() → set of legal action types
  round_state.raise_bounds()  → (min_raise, max_raise) total amounts

Card format: 'Ah' = Ace of hearts, 'Td' = Ten of diamonds
Suits: h/d/s/c, Ranks: 2-9, T, J, Q, K, A
